Welcome to InfinitiAir!

The Premier VA for Pilots

InfinitiAir Virtual Airlines was founded by 5 individuals who are dedicated to serving the pilots. Us 5 are devoted to making the best flight simulator experience possible and listening to our pilots. We won't boast statistics, because we have very little as we started this idea in late 2017. We will boast an unrivaled commitment to most importantly, our PILOTS.

Our team of founders that are very dedicated to this VA. We also are building our Staff team that will include Events, Marketing, HUB Managers, Webmaster and any other position that enhance the virtual pilot experience.

Your flight will be recorded using our acars system, once logged in you can find a direct download in the downloads section. If you haven’t done so already, you will need the FSUIPC or XPUIPC client installed in your sim for the acars to read your flight. We have posted a link on the downloads page for you to download and install. Bid for your desired flight in the schedules. Open your sim and acars; your bidded flight should be there ready to go once you login.

We have no restriction on the airline and callsign you use. You can follow what you have bidded on or simply use your IFA callsign. When connected to the network we ask to include in your remark the ifinitiair.com URL to represent the wonderful VA you are apart of. Maintain the upmost respect and conduct when speaking with ATC. Enjoy yourself and Welcome to the InfinitiAir Team.
