FAQs

Is there a rank system?

No, sky is the limit with routes and aircraft flown. We're all equall and share the same statute.

How can I connect to InfinitiAirVA's Discord?

You can click the Connect button on the Home Page in the Discord widget, or you can use this link. https://discord.gg/UMswrX

Which Flight Simulators can I use?

In order to have the ACARS flight reporting program work, you must have a legal copy of FSX, FSX Steam, All versions of Prepare3D, or X-Plane.

Can I use a different aircraft in place of the one specified on the scheduled route I bid?

Absolutely! We offer a recommended aircraft for the route specified as a guide. If you want to substitute that aircraft for one you would like to fly, you can do that! We do recommend something comparable, though, as you don’t want to be taking a heavy airliner into a small airport!

How can I connect to SmartCARS?

You connect to our smartCARS with the same login that you would use to login to your crewcenter.

We appreciate your effort in looking for answers here on the FAQ page and we strive to keep it updated. If you found this page lacking in need information, we would appreciate an email through the contact us link with a brief description.
